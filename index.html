About Me

I am majoring in Industrial Engineering with a minor in Economics

Industrial Engineering is about designing and improving existing systems for every industry. I was drawn to it through my desire to eliminate waste through increasing productivity such as looking at a fast food line and thinking about how I can get my food faster.
